About

The TEA Coder, opened its first branch in Tarlac city with a simple mission: to share a taste of Hong Kong to the Filipino people. TEA Coder serves Hong Kong-style milktea with premium ingredients and generous toppings. But wait, what is Hong Kong-style tea anyway? Unlike the tea you’re used to, Hong Kong-style tea is very delicious and affordable with different flavours and topings. A generous serving of the freshest, highest-quality ingredients, and you get—simply put—great Hong Kong-style tea.
